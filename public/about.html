Welcome to "Community-Connect" of Barangay San Antonio De Padua I Management Information System (BMIS)

Welcome to the official portal of San Antonio De Padua I Management Information System (BMIS). Our BMIS is designed to streamline and enhance the administrative processes of our barangay, providing efficient services to our residents and stakeholders.

About San Antonio De Padua I

San Antonio De Padua I is a vibrant community nestled in Dasmariñas, Cavite. With a population of 3,875, our barangay is home to diverse cultures, traditions, and livelihoods. Our dedicated barangay officials and staff work tirelessly to ensure the welfare and development of our community.

Mission

Our mission is to foster a safe, inclusive, and sustainable community by providing responsive governance, quality services, and opportunities for growth and development.

Vision

Our vision is to be a model barangay where residents live harmoniously, empowered to achieve their fullest potential amidst a thriving and resilient community.

Our Services

  • Barangay Documents: Apply for and process various barangay documents such as barangay clearances, permits, certifications, and endorsements.
  • Community Programs: Stay informed about upcoming community programs, events, and initiatives aimed at promoting social welfare, health, and education.
  • Incident Reporting: Report incidents, complaints, or concerns within the barangay through our online reporting system. Our barangay officials are committed to addressing issues promptly and effectively.
  • Community Directory: Access contact information for barangay officials, departments, and key stakeholders within our barangay.
  • Community News and Updates: Stay up-to-date with the latest news, announcements, and developments happening in our barangay.

How to Use BMIS

Navigating through our BMIS is simple and user-friendly. Use the menu options or search function to find the information or service you need. Our system is accessible 24/7, providing convenience and accessibility to our residents.

Get Involved

We encourage residents to actively participate in barangay activities and initiatives. Whether it's volunteering for community projects, attending barangay assemblies, or providing feedback on our services, your involvement is vital to the progress and development of our barangay.

Contact Us

Have questions, feedback, or suggestions? Feel free to reach out to us through our contact information provided. Our barangay office is always ready to assist you with your inquiries or concerns.
